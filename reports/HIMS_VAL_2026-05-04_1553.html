  • Regulatory uncertainty is too binary to project 5 years. Either GLP-1 framework stabilizes (path A, ~75% of forward EBITDA) or it doesn't (path B, ~50% of forward EBITDA). Averaging these in a deterministic 5Y DCF would mislead.

Sanity check on FCF instead: FY25 free cash flow of $57.4M (vs $198M FY24) reflects the capex spike. Normalized FCF (using $241M FY25 capex normalized to 30% of EBITDA per High Growth steady-state = $95M) would imply FY25 FCF of ~$200M+. At current $7B EV, that's ~3% normalized FCF yield — reasonable for High Growth. Suggests SOTP base case is not aggressive on FCF assumptions.
