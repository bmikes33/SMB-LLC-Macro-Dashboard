§6 — SBC Treatment

Per skill default: Bull case adds back SBC (street standard, treats SBC as non-cash). Base & Bear strict (SBC stays embedded in EBITDA as a real cost). META TTM SBC is $22.3B = 10.4% of revenue — a meaningful real cost that materially impacts the gap between bull-addback and base/bear-strict treatments. SBC ramped sharply in Q1'26 ($6.0B vs $4.1B = +45% YoY) reflecting AI talent hires.

The Q1'26 SBC step-up to $6B/quarter ($24B annualized) is a real headwind to economic earnings. Bull case "EBITDA + SBC addback" treats this as non-cash; base/bear treat it as a cash cost. The honest read: at $24B annualized, SBC dilution + tax leakage is meaningful, especially with planned 8,000-person headcount cut in May likely driving one-time restructuring charges that offset compensation savings near-term.

  • Lifecycle stage: FoA = Mature Growth (user-confirmed); RL = Young Growth.

Key Risks & Caveats

  • Capex normalization risk: Base case implicitly assumes capex/EBITDA reverts to ~30% by Y5. Mark Zuckerberg explicitly said on the Q1 call that they "aren't optimizing for a specific cash flow level." If capex stays elevated longer than Y5, fair value compresses by ~$50-100/share.
  • EU LPA headwind: Susan Li flagged Q2'26 will see "larger" revenue impact from less personalized ads in Europe. Already factored into Q2 guide of $58-61B. If LPA enforcement broadens beyond EU, base growth assumption (+26.4%) could prove optimistic.
  • Youth-related litigation: Management noted "additional trials scheduled for this year in the U.S., which may ultimately result in a material loss." Not modeled. Could be a discrete tail risk.
  • SBC ramp: Q1'26 SBC of $6.0B is a meaningful real cost. Bull case adds it back; base/bear keep it embedded. Real economic value is closer to base/bear treatment.
  • RL trajectory: Continues to operate at $19B+ annual loss. Management explicitly said this remains expected for foreseeable future. Not a thesis break, but worth monitoring quarterly.
  • Stagflation regime context: This SOTP is unadjusted for the macro regime. The user's macro framework (stagflation, weakening growth + accelerating inflation) is a headwind to ad market durability that the base case does not penalize. Apply regime overlay manually.
