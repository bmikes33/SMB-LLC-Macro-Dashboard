▸ The Real Story — What's Actually Driving the Beat
01Customer Diversification Thesis Is Working
Top 2 customer concentration: 67% → 58% YoY. Rest-of-Market revenue: +78% YoY, now 42% of mix (was 33%). This was the bear case for years — "they're a Comcast/Charter prop trade." Q1 actively dismantles it. Bookings were >50% Rest-of-Market in the quarter.
02Backlog Is Now the Story
$582.1M backlog + deferred (+87% YoY). CFO disclosed 60% converts within 12 months = ~$349M of visible revenue. Against FY26 guide midpoint of $485M, that's ~72% of guide already in backlog. Eliminates near-term execution risk — the raise is well-supported.
03cOS™ Adoption + DOCSIS 4.0 Tailwind
150 cOS customers · 45.7M cable modems served. New DOCSIS 4.0 wins called out. Fiber product mix >14% of Appliance & Integration revenue over trailing year. The MSO upgrade cycle (DOCSIS 3.1 → 4.0) is the multi-year tailwind — Harmonic is the only pure-play virtualized vendor at scale.
04Video Divestiture On the Glidepath
$145M cash to MediaKind, expected Q2 close. APA executed March 20, French works council consultation complete. Net cash post-close: $109M cash + $145M proceeds − $108M LT debt = ~$146M net cash position. This is a pure-play Broadband co. by Q3 with a fortress balance sheet for the size.
05OCF Down Hard — Working Capital Reversal
Operating cash flow: $31.7M Q1'26 vs $83.6M Q1'25. The Q1'25 was inflated by a massive $79.6M AR collection. Strip that out and core OCF is actually up. Not a real flag, but it'll show ugly in YoY screens. Worth flagging if anyone asks.
06Buybacks Continue — $43M in Q1
Bought 4.2M shares ($43M, ~3.8% of float). $122M of the $200M auth complete. Share count 111.2M → 108.5M. Mgmt explicit goal: at minimum offset SBC dilution. Note: debt was paid down and re-drawn during the quarter ($55M each way) — likely a treasury rotation, not a real net add.
